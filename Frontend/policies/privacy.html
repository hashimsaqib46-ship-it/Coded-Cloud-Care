Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

At Cloud Care Systems, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your institutional and personal information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, professional email, phone number, and institution details.
  • Operational Data: Patient records, laboratory results, pharmacy inventory (all handled with high security).
  • Payment Data: Handled by secure third-party processors. We do not store full credit card numbers on our servers.
  • Usage Data: Technical logs, browser type, and interactions with our platform to improve service quality.

3. How We Use Information

Your data is used specifically for:

  • Providing healthcare management functionalities.
  • Ensuring secure access and tenant isolation.
  • Processing transactions and billing.
  • Technical support and system improvements.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted "at rest" and "in transit" using SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Tenant Isolation: Each institution’s data is cryptographically separated.
  • Access Control: Granular permission levels for your staff (Doctors, Admin, Lab Techs).

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share information with partners essential for service delivery (e.g., PayFast for billing, cloud hosting providers) under strict confidentiality agreements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or request deletion of your institutional data at any time, subject to local legal requirements for medical record retention.

Privacy concerns? Contact us at privacy@codedclouds.org
