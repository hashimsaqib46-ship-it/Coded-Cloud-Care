Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time via the admin dashboard or by contacting our billing team. Cancellations will take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. You will retain access to the system until your current paid period expires.

2. Refund Eligibility

We offer a 7-day Money Back Guarantee for first-time subscribers. If you are not satisfied with the system, you can request a full refund within the first 7 days of your initial payment.

Refunds are not applicable in the following scenarios:

  • Renewals for existing monthly or yearly subscriptions.
  • Accounts suspended due to violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Setup fees or custom development costs already incurred.
  • Refund requests made after the 7-day window.

3. Refund Process

To request a refund, please email billing@codedclouds.org with your Sales Number and reason for refund. Once approved, the refund will be processed back to your original payment method (Credit Card, Bank Account, or Wallet) within 7-10 business days.

4. Duplicate Payments

In case of unintentional duplicate payments due to technical issues, we will issue a full refund for the duplicate transaction immediately upon verification.

5. Data Retention after Cancellation

Upon cancellation or non-payment, we retain your data for 30 days. After this period, if the account is not reactivated, all data may be permanently purged from our secure servers to protect privacy. We recommend exporting your records before the 30-day grace period ends.

Billing concerns? Contact us at billing@codedclouds.org
