Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Digital Delivery

Cloud Care Systems is a cloud-based Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) platform. No physical goods are shipped. All services and software modules are delivered digitally via our secure web interface.

2. Account Activation

Upon successful payment confirmation through our integrated payment gateways (e.g., PayFast), your account credentials and system access will be provisioned. Standard activation time is:

  • Instant: For basic monthly/yearly subscription tiers.
  • 24-48 Hours: For custom Enterprise setups requiring specific data migration or domain mapping.

3. Delivery Confirmation

Once your system is ready, you will receive an automated "System Ready" email containing your administrative login URL and temporary credentials. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive this within 1 hour of payment.

4. Hardware Requirements

While we do not ship hardware, the software is optimized for delivery on all modern web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge). For laboratory or pharmacy modules, any standard thermal printer or barcode scanner can be integrated via your local browser settings.

5. Geographic Availability

Our digital services are available globally. However, specialized module compliance (like taxation or regional health forms) is currently optimized for the Pakistani healthcare market.

Questions regarding delivery? Contact us at support@codedclouds.org
