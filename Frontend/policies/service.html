Service Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Service Overview

Cloud Care Systems provide advanced healthcare management solutions including Hospital Management Systems (HMS), Electronic Medical Records (EMR), Pharmacy Management, and Laboratory Management modules. Our services are delivered as a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) model.

2. Onboarding & Implementation

Upon subscription, our team will provide:

  • System environment setup for your institution.
  • Initial data configuration and tenant isolation.
  • Staff training sessions (as per the selected plan).
  • API integration support for third-party diagnostics or payment gateways.

3. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

We strive for 99.9% uptime. Maintenance windows are typically scheduled during off-peak hours, and clients are notified at least 24 hours in advance. Emergency maintenance may be performed without prior notice to protect data integrity.

4. Technical Support

Support is available via:

  • Email: support@codedclouds.org
  • Phone: +966-557385262
  • In-app ticketing system during business hours (Monday - Friday).

5. User Responsibilities

Clients are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their login credentials and ensuring that all data entered into the system complies with local healthcare regulations and data protection laws.

6. Modifications to Service

Coded Clouds reserves the right to update features, modules, and user interfaces to improve performance and security. Major changes will be communicated via release notes on the dashboard.
